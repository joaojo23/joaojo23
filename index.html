EVENT CEREMONY OVERVIEW

Welcome to The EASTC Seventh Annually Convocation Ceremony where it is done annually before the graduation ceremony as special occassion to honor our graduating students for their hard work and dedication. Students are provided with awards and certification due to their class performance which encourages to increase more effort to students in studying hard and attaining awards. You're all Welcome.
